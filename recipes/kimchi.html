Kimchi

this recipes tells you how to make korean kimchi using the traditional style by adding rice porridge to help fermentation

Ingredients

  • Napa Cabbage
  • Korean red chili pepper
  • Ginger
  • Garlic
  • Spring onion
  • Daikon
  • salt
  • glutinous rice flour
  • sugar
  • Korean shrimp paste

Steps

  1. salt napa cabbage with salt, the amount of which is 2.5% of the total weight of the cabbage
  2. leave the cabbage for 2 hours and a lot of water will come out of the cabbage
  3. make a rice porridge with rice flour, sugar and water
  4. mix rice porridge with garlic and ginger paste, shrimp paste
  5. spread the paste on the leaves of the cabbage
